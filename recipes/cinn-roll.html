Cinnamon Rolls

Description

These easy cinnamon rolls are perfect for yeast beginners because they only require 1 rise. You have a few options for toppings. The recipe below includes a simple cream cheese icing, but we also love these with the coffee icing or vanilla icing that’s included in the recipe Notes below.

Ingredients

    For the Dough:

  • 1. 1 cup warm milk (about 110°F or 43°C)
  • 2. 2 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3. 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 4. 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 5. 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 7. 2 large eggs

    For the Filling:

  • 1. 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2. 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 3. 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened

    For the Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 1. 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2. 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 3. 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 4. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 5. Pinch of salt

Steps

    For the Cinnamon Filling:

  • In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and cinnamon.
  • After the dough has risen, roll it out on a floured surface into a large rectangle.
  • Spread softened butter over the dough, and then sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly.
  • Starting from one long edge, roll the dough into a log.
  • Cut the log into equal-sized rolls, about 12-15 rolls.
  • Place the rolls in a greased baking pan.
  • Cover and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.

    • For the Baking:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes or until they are golden brown.

    • For the Cheese Frosting:

  • While the rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a bowl, beat together softened butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  • Once the rolls are out of the oven, let them cool slightly before spreading the cream cheese frosting over the top.
  • Serve warm and enjoy your homemade cinnamon rolls!
