Ube Cheesecake

Description

Ube cheesecake, also known as purple yam cheesecake, is a Filipino cheesecake made with a base of crushed graham crackers and an upper layer of cream cheese and ube halaya. It can be prepared baked or simply refrigerated. Like other ube desserts in the Philippines, it is characteristically purple in color.

    • For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 3 packages (24 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup ube halaya (purple yam jam)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Purple food coloring (optional, for a deeper color)

Steps

    Crust Preperation:

  • Preheat your oven to 325째F (163째C).
  • In a bowl, mix together graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar until well combined.
  • Press the mixture into the bottom of a greased 9-inch springform pan to create the crust.
  • Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes or until it sets. Remove and let it cool while you prepare the filling.

    • Cheesecake Filling:

  • In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth.
  • Add sugar and continue to beat until well combined.
  • Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  • Mix in the ube halaya, flour, sour cream, and vanilla extract until the batter is smooth and well incorporated. Add purple food coloring if desired for a deeper color.
  • Pour the cheesecake filling over the prepared crust.

    • Baking:

  • Tap the pan on the counter to remove any air bubbles.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes or until the center is set and the top is lightly browned.
  • Turn off the oven, crack the oven door, and let the cheesecake cool for 1 hour inside the oven.
  • Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  • Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

    • Serving:

  • Once fully chilled, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan.
  • Slice and serve chilled. Optionally, garnish with whipped cream or additional ube halaya.
