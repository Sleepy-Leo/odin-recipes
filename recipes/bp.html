Banana Pudding

Description

Discover the art of crafting our Banana Pudding, a delightful dessert made with precision and care. We start with layers of velvety vanilla custard, carefully placing ripe banana slices, and interspersing them with soft vanilla wafers. This isn't just a dessert; it's a journey through the process of creating a comforting classic. The harmonious blend of creamy custard and natural sweetness from the bananas is achieved through a meticulous approach. Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-urpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1 box (11oz) vanilla
  • 2 cups whipped cream (or whipped topping)

Steps

  • 1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together sugar, flour, and salt.
  • 2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, and then add them to the sugar mixture in the saucepan.
  • 3. Gradually whisk in the milk until the mixture is smooth.
  • 4. Place the saucepan over medium heat, and cook the mixture while stirring constantly until it thickens (about 8-10 minutes). Make sure it doesn't boil.
  • 5. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  • 6. Allow the custard to cool slightly.
  • 7. In a trifle dish or a serving bowl, start layering the banana pudding. Begin with a layer of vanilla wafers, followed by a layer of sliced bananas, and then a layer of custard. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of custard on top.
  • 8. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
  • 9. Before serving, top the banana pudding with a generous layer of whipped cream or whipped topping.
  • 10. Optionally, garnish with additional banana slices or crumbled vanilla wafers.
